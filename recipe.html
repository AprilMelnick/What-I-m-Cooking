Mango Pickle Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 40ml sunflower oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 70g mango pickle finely chopped
  • 125g frozen peas
  • 30g roasted cashews roughly chopped

Serves 2

Directions

  1. First, fry the garlic. Put a large saute pan on medium heat and, once hot, add 40ml oil and the garlic. Fry for two to three minutes until lightly golden and starting to crisp up. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside on kitchen paper

  2. Add the pickle to the oil and cook, stirring frequently, for one minute until fragrant. Add the rice and spring onions to the pan, stir gently to mix, then increase the heat to medium-high and cook for 10-15 minutes, shaking the pan occasionally. Do not be tempted to stir too often as you want the rice at the bottom of the pan to get crispy.

  3. Stir in the vegetables with a pinch of salt and a generous grind of black pepper. Cook for a further two to three minutes, shaking the pan occasionally. Take off the heat and set aside.

  4. Mix half of the reserved spring onion greens, fried garlic and the cashews into the rice and spoon on to a platter and serve.
